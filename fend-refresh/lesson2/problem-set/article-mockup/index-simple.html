I like my eggs over easy. Here's my recipe:

  1. Drip a little bit of olive oil on the warm pan.
  2. Crack a large egg into the pan carefully so the yolk stays intact.
  3. Drench the egg in my hot sauce du jour.
  4. Cook until the egg white is white and firm (usually no more than two or three minutes).

To turn it into a sandwhich, I slide the egg into an English muffin that I've toasted to a golden brown. If I'm feeling fancy, I'll put a slice of Muenster cheese on top.
